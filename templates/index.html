About Bagasbas Beach Resort

Bagasbas Beach Resort offers a relaxing coastal experience with beautiful scenery, exciting activities, and memorable events. Perfect for family outings, group trips, and weekend escapes.

🏝Resort story

Bagasbas started as a quiet coastal retreat for local families and has grown into a welcoming beach destination focused on comfort, community, and memorable seaside experiences.

✨Amenities
  • Beachfront cottages and shaded lounge areas
  • Open event spaces and activity zones
  • Food kiosks and refreshment stalls
  • Public restrooms, parking, and shower areas
📋Rules / policies
  • Observe cleanliness and use designated trash bins
  • No glass bottles in beach activity areas
  • Follow lifeguard instructions and safety markers
  • Respect quiet hours after evening events
